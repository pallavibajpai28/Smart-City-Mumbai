Why You Should Visit Mumbai City!

Bombay, India’s New York, the City that never sleeps or the City of Dreams. Mumbai is known by many names. With a population of over 1.2 crores, Mumbai is one of the largest metropolitan cities in the world.Mumbai, the heartbeat of Maharashtra and even India, is the financial capital of the country and the capital city of the state of Maharashtra. Seven islands came together in the Arabian Sea and formed this great mass of land. The city is the commercial and entertainment centre of the country and is home to the globally acclaimed ‘Bollywood’ movies.
